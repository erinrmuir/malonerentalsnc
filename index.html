The perfect place to call home, this bungalow is a beatiful remodel, conveniently walkable to all your favorite downtown spots. Enjoy the morning sunshine through the tall living room windows, tap a draft in the kitchen when you need it most, or relax under the market lights on the back patio. The thoughtful restoration includes exposed beams, a wood stove and a number of style choices that create a premium blend of efficiency and character.

Located in Historic Boylan Heights, the home sits adjacent to a well-manicured neighborhood park and is just a short walk to the best views in the city on Boylan Bridge. Allow this welcoming neighborhood to charm you and embrace the energy of a truly urban lifestyle.
